About the Project  >

The Magpies team are young energetic team of IT Students who have embarked on a new and innovative project to build an application that will create and improve the way medical appointments are organised

The application, Magpie Medical® is in design stage and will be available towards the end of Q1-2022 on Andriod and IOS Mobiles. It is a secure application using a login and password to authenticate. It will connect to wearable devices such as Samsung Galaxy Watch and Apple Watch to report blood pressure, heart rate, ECG etc. for the General Practitioner to understand the key vitals for their patients. It will connect to smart scales to record weight, BMI and body trends. The app will store your appointments and allow you to track back the visits made to doctors. It will store your prescriptions and send them to the pharmacy of choice to dispense medicines as recommended.

This application will reduce the time spent on waiting at doctorâ€™s dispensary and give the general practitioners more time to view patients from the comfort of their homes.

As emergency and GP after-hours struggle to accommodate the patients during winter times due to the flu season, this product will play a key part in ensuring medical attention is available for people that are in need. It will also become popular when cities are in lockdown and travelling to see a GP needs to be avoided.
