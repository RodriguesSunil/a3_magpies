Network Engineer

Job ID A12456 Sydney    Brisbane    Newcastle    Perth Educational Information Technology Sector Full Time 12-month Contract

Who We are?

Magpies is a group of students from RMIT who are preparing to launch a new App called Magpie Medical into the market. The app will provide a convenient and more time friendly way for people without medical emergencies to interact with a local General Practitioner from the comfort of their home. The app is scheduled for go live end of Q1-2022. It is your opportunity to work with a team of experts in deployment of an innovative application that will benefit people throughout Australia.

How do you fit in the company?

Magpies is looking for an experienced Network Engineer who can work unsupervised on a 12-month contract, building the company’s firewall and setting up the Routers and Network Switches. You will design, deploy, and manage WAN, Cloud, Check Point and Cisco Systems as per the requirements.

Your Profile

If you are a motivated, self-starter who can work remotely and unsupervised delivering network-based solutions we would like to speak to you. You will have good communication skills, be punctual and work harmoniously within a team environment. You will identify risks and put mitigation plans in place. An IT degree or extensive Network Certification is what we are looking for you to have. You will have authorisation to work in Australia and be willing to undergo police clearance if requested.

Your key skills include but are not limited to:

  • Associate level certification in the field of Firewalls Routers, Network Switching or Load Balancing.
  • Over 5 years’ experience in network security, firewalls, and networking.
  • Professional level understanding of network communications protocols
  • Work unsupervised and remotely ensuring tasks are completed as per the project plan.
  • Experience in troubleshooting Cisco, Citrix firewalls, Check Point, Palo Alto firewalls.
  • Experience in designing and developing DMZ designs.

How to Apply?

To apply for the role, email your CV along with a cover letter detailing what you can bring to our organisation and assist in the deployment of this application. Send your completed applications to: recruitment@magpies.com


Application close:01 December 2021
Shortlisting of the applications will commence on the 05 Dec 2021.
