Magpies Group Reflection

Magpies Group Reflection
The Magpies group worked hard to get this assignment completed within the timelines. There was a bit of confusion with the Assignment 5 dates that caused us to panic, but we achieved the completion by reshuffling our activities

We learnt about the group from assignment 2 and hence it was easy for us to quickly build up a Gantt Chart and assign activities to names. We planned to start early but due to work commitments we had a bit of delay in getting started. This team works together well and copes up quite well when under pressure. This is attributed to the diverse age group and the experience of the people in the group. It is a lively group and meeting are enjoyable. We have had good attendance given the time difference and the locations. When people are not available there is always communication and our recordings have helped those absent to see what was discussed and get back on track. As a group this assignment has thought us a lot of new items and this will improve our knowledge and skills as we progress through our IT journey.

Most of our time was spent on the project and collecting information about how we could make the project successful. We did not use GitHub to the full extent, as Teams was used instead. Teams was easy to use and having access to Teams on the phone gave the group the flexibility to review and check what was being done by the group.

The item of improvement if we are to carry out another assignment, would be to do a look ahead plan earlier than what we currently did. This would have given us more time on the assignment and would have been less stressful to the group. However, there was no compromise to the content that was produced by the group.

The surprising thing in doing this assignment was identifying the steps that we required to get the application built and into production. There is a lot of work that goes into development and launch of an application. It gave us an appreciation of the work IT professional do and at the same time gave the group more confidence that this was an achievable target.

The one thing that we all learned working together was that for the group to be successful, each member of the group must know what is required and have an insight into the other persons work. This helps take over in case a member is not available to carry out their tasks. Hence as a group we must learn from each other and impart our knowledge to the group, so that together we become successful.
